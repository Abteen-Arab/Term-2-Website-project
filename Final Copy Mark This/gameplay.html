Game Play

Cards

Players have the freedom of chosing their desired deck of 8 cards, which can be combinations of spells and characters.

Intro

The eulogized tower rush game sets players in an arena where they clash each other in a 1v1 or 2v2 format. Clash Royale employs many similar characters operated in Clash of Clans, while settling them in a dissimilar playing field.

Maps

The maps all possess similar outlines, with two lanes and 3 towers. There are two Queen towers that attack the opponents characters and there is also the important king tower which also attacks characters.

Objective

The objective of Clash Royale is to gain more crowns that your opponent before the time runs out. Crowns could either be earned from destroying the Queen tower or destroying the King tower which automatically ends the games.
