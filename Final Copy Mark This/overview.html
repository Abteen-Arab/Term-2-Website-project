Overview

Introduction

Clash Royale is a free real-time tower strategy game that has been credited for restructuring the mobile gaming market.

Effects

The success of Clash Royale allowed for the creation ofan entirely new genre of tower strategy card game in the mobile industry.

Success

After its release on March 2, 2016, the game succeeded expectations, earning over a billion dollars in revenue by the end of its first year.
